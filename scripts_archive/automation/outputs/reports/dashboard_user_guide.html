Zen Medicine — Care Coordinator & Provider User Guide

This guide explains how to use the Zen Medicine Streamlit dashboard for Care Coordinators (CC) and Care Providers (CP). It references the latest automation screenshots and covers common workflows, filters, and tips.

Prerequisites

  • Valid login: CC and CP role accounts
  • Streamlit app running (Local preview)
  • Use the sidebar for navigation and filters

Login

Use your role-based credentials on the left sidebar login panel. After authentication, the app will render dashboards based on your primary role.

Care Coordinator — Dashboard Overview

Purpose: triage and coordinate daily patient follow-ups and phone reviews.

What you see

  • KPI cards: Today’s calls completed, Pending phone reviews, Escalations.
  • Work queues: My Patients table, Phone Reviews queue, Team Management.

Key panels & columns

  • My Patients: Patient, Last Visit, Next Task, Priority, Assigned Provider, Status.
  • Phone Reviews: Call Type, Due Date, Priority, Assigned To, Outcome.
  • Team Management: Staff, Role, Load (# patients), Pending tasks.

Filters & date range

  • Use sidebar filters: role, provider, status, priority, date range, search.
  • Date range affects KPIs and queue contents; clear filters to reset.

Common actions

  • Click a row to open patient detail; record outcomes and update the next task.
  • Reassign tasks via Team Management when workloads are imbalanced.
  • Use Phone Reviews to batch-process today’s calls.

Care Provider — Dashboard Overview

Purpose: manage assigned patients, complete phone reviews, and work through clinical tasks.

Landing (Provider Dashboard)

  • KPI cards: Active panel, Tasks due today, Overdue items.
  • Highlights: alerts for urgent follow-ups and escalations.

My Patients

  • Columns: Patient, Last Visit, Next Task, Priority, Workflow Stage, Status.
  • Actions: open patient detail, record notes, advance workflow stage.
  • Filters: search by patient/provider; date range narrows visit/task windows.

Phone Reviews

  • Queue shows review calls due by date, with priority and call type.
  • Click into a call to document outcome and schedule next follow-up.
  • Batch-process using filters (e.g., due today, high priority).

Task Review

  • Columns: Task, Patient, Due, Priority, Assigned, Status.
  • Use status filters to focus on open/overdue items; mark completed when done.
  • Color legend: red = overdue; amber = due soon; green = completed.

General Tips

  • Use sidebar filters to narrow scope; clear them if counts look off.
  • Most tables support sort by clicking column headers.
  • If a tab is disabled, it’s likely not part of your role’s workflow.

Quick Tips

  • Use sidebar filters to refine patients and tasks.
  • Switch tabs to access role-specific workflows.
  • If a tab is unavailable for your role, the app will disable or hide it.
  • For assistance, use the Help button at the bottom of the left sidebar.

Troubleshooting

  • If screenshots appear blank, refresh the page or re-run the capture automation.
  • Ensure you are logged in with the correct role.
  • Contact support if tabs are missing or filters don’t respond.

Step-by-Step Workflow Guides

Coordinator — My Patients (Step-by-Step)

  1. Login as hector@myhealthteam.org (CC).
  2. On the Coordinator Dashboard, set date range and filters (provider, status, priority) as needed.
  3. Open the My Patients table; sort by Priority or Next Task.
  4. Click a patient row to open details; review Last Visit and Next Task.
  5. Record notes and update the Next Task (e.g., schedule follow-up call).
  6. Save changes; the table updates status and counts.

Coordinator — Phone Reviews (Step-by-Step)

  1. Login as hector@myhealthteam.org.
  2. Open the Phone Reviews tab or section.
  3. Filter to "Due today" or high priority calls.
  4. Click a call item to review context; document outcome.
  5. Schedule next follow-up and assign if needed.
  6. Return to the queue and repeat until completed.

Note: Coordinator-specific Phone Reviews screenshot will be captured on the next automation run; behavior mirrors the Provider queue shown below.

Provider — My Patients (Step-by-Step)

  1. Login as angela@myhealthteam.org (CP).
  2. Open the My Patients tab.
  3. Use filters to narrow to your assigned panel or search by patient.
  4. Sort by Priority or Next Task; click a patient to open details.
  5. Record clinical notes and update workflow stage and next task.
  6. Save; verify status and KPIs reflect changes.

Provider — Phone Reviews (Step-by-Step)

  1. Login as angela@myhealthteam.org.
  2. Open the Phone Reviews tab; filter by due date (e.g., today).
  3. Select a call item; document outcome (completed, rescheduled, unable to reach).
  4. Schedule the next follow-up and set priority.
  5. Mark completed; return to the queue to process remaining calls.

Provider — Task Review (Step-by-Step)

  1. Login as angela@myhealthteam.org.
  2. Open the Task Review tab.
  3. Use status filters (Open, Overdue) and sort by Due date.
  4. Open a task; review patient context; complete or update as appropriate.
  5. Apply the color legend to triage: red = overdue, amber = due soon, green = done.

Provider — Onboarding Queue & Initial TV Visits (Step-by-Step)

  1. Login as angela@myhealthteam.org.
  2. Open the Onboarding Queue & Initial TV Visits tab.
  3. Filter to new patients awaiting initial visits; sort by scheduled date.
  4. Open patient detail; review readiness; schedule or confirm tele-visit.
  5. Record outcomes; ensure initial TV completed and next steps set.

Note: This tab’s screenshot will be captured in the next automation run if present; some environments may hide it based on role or data availability.

Troubleshooting

  • If screenshots appear blank, refresh the page or re-run the capture automation.
  • Ensure you are logged in with the correct role.
  • Contact support if tabs are missing or filters don’t respond.
