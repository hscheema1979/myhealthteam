Task Entry — Coordinator

Coordinator Task Notes — What to write:
  • Contact method and participants (phone, in-person, facility staff).
  • Purpose and brief summary of actions.
  • Outcome and any escalations or provider notifications.
  • Next steps and due dates; who owns them.
Example: "Intake call with daughter and facility RN. Confirmed contact info; requested records (med list, discharge summary). Escalated med discrepancy to provider via portal message. Next: schedule PCP tele-visit; due in 3 days."
Saved to coordinator tasks in the live app; required fields enforced. Disabled here for demonstration.

  • Persistence: Writes to coordinator task records for reporting.
